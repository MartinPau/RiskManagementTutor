<h1 class="font-headline-xl text-headline-xl text-on-surface mb-8 text-balance">The Risk Management Process</h1>
<p class="font-body-lg text-body-lg text-on-surface-variant text-balance">
                    Establishing a systematic application of management policies, procedures, and practices to the tasks of analyzing, evaluating, controlling, and monitoring risk throughout the total product life cycle.
                </p>
</header>
<!-- Main Reading Layout (Editorial Asymmetry) -->
<div class="grid grid-cols-4 md:grid-cols-12 gap-gutter mb-16">
<!-- Left Column: Context & Definitions -->
<div class="col-span-4 md:col-span-4 space-y-12 pr-0 md:pr-8 border-r-0 md:border-r border-outline-variant">
<section>
<h3 class="font-label-sm text-label-sm text-on-surface mb-4 border-b border-outline-variant pb-2 uppercase">1.0 Scope</h3>
<p class="font-body-md text-body-md text-on-surface-variant">
                            This process applies to all medical devices, including Software as a Medical Device (SaMD), and describes the ongoing process for identifying hazards associated with a medical device, estimating and evaluating the associated risks, controlling these risks, and monitoring the effectiveness of the controls.
                        </p>
</section>
<section class="bg-surface-container-high p-6 rounded ghost-border">
<span class="material-symbols-outlined text-primary mb-4" data-icon="info">info</span>
<h4 class="font-headline-lg text-headline-lg text-on-surface mb-2">Key Definition</h4>
<p class="font-body-md text-body-md text-on-surface-variant mb-4">
<strong class="text-on-surface">Harm:</strong> Physical injury or damage to the health of people, or damage to property or the environment.
                        </p>
<p class="font-body-md text-body-md text-on-surface-variant">
<strong class="text-on-surface">Hazard:</strong> Potential source of harm.
                        </p>
</section>
</div>
<!-- Right Column: Core Content & Matrix -->
<div class="col-span-4 md:col-span-8 space-y-16 md:pl-8">
<section>
<h2 class="font-headline-lg text-headline-lg text-on-surface mb-6">Risk Acceptability Criteria</h2>
<p class="font-body-md text-body-md text-on-surface-variant mb-6 text-balance">
                            Before evaluating the risk of individual identified hazards, the manufacturer must establish criteria for risk acceptability. These criteria must be based on applicable national or regional regulations, relevant international standards, and take into account generally acknowledged state of the art.
                        </p>
<p class="font-body-md text-body-md text-on-surface-variant mb-8 text-balance">
                            The evaluation relies on a combination of severity (the measure of the possible consequences of a hazard) and probability (the likelihood of the occurrence of harm).
                        </p>
